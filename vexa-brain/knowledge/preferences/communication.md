# Communication Preferences

## Response Style
- Prefers exact date and time for time-based information
- Wants search queries to be simple and direct
- Expects assistant to recognize contextual phrases as single words for accurate search
- When searching emails, wants exact date and time of receipt
- Prefers brief, to-the-point responses

## Information Formatting
- Likes structured, clear information
- Appreciates when assistant connects answers to his tech background
- Prefers specificity over vagueness
- uses VS Code for development
