## Telugu Phrases
- **Tappakunda**: "definitely", "surely", "without fail" — used for strong agreement
- **Khaali chesi**: "emptying/clearing out" — implies urgency, "get it done quickly"
- **Aina kooda**: "even then", "nevertheless" — emphasizes persistence despite conditions
- **Nak telusu**: "I know" — assertive confirmation of awareness
- **Aratipamula**: Used in conjunction with formal contexts
